Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology, Patiala is organizing North India's largest Annual Inter-Collegiate festival, Saturnalia'22 from 24 November to 27 November 2022. The event will include a plethora of competitions in various fields, ending with star performers gracing us with their grand performances. 

From graceful Classical to Western dance,  Saturnalia is an elegant collection of exceptional events. There will be feature competitions of bands from various colleges, a riot of musical symphonies, and finally a stage to showcase your quirkiest talents. The three-day event will also provide students with various exciting Technical competitions.

We sincerely request your enthusiastic participation
