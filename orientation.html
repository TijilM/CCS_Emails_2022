Orientation 2022

9 October | Sunday| 6 PM | LT 101

Creative Computing Society is one of the oldest and the most prestigious societies of Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology. With the knowledge and experience of 18 years, the society aims to create and promote a healthy coding culture at TIET.
Further enhancing our indomitable legacy, CCS  is organising its Orientation Programme 2022 on Sunday, 9th October 2022.
The event will start at 6:00 PM  and will take place in LT101.  The objective of the event is to provide freshers with a clear view of the goals, objectives and recruitment process of the society.
